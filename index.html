1. High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) - Fastest Results

  • Alternates between short bursts of intense effort and rest
  • Boosts heart health, endurance, and burns fat in less time
  • 10-15 min sessions highly effective

Example 20-minute HIIT workout:

Jumping jacks 30 sec
Rest 15 sec
High knees 30 sec
Rest 15 sec
Burpees 30 sec
Rest 15 sec
Mountain climbers 30 sec
Rest 15 sec
*Repeat 3-4 rounds*

2. Tempo Runs or Jog-Sprints

  • Builds speed and endurance
  • Pushes aerobic threshold

Example:

  • Warm-up: 5 min jog
  • 1 min fast run / 1 min walk (x6-10)
  • Cool down: 3 min light jog or walk

3. Stair or Hill Sprints

  • Combines power and cardio
  • Sprint 15-30 sec, walk down, repeat 6-10x

  • Keeps heart rate elevated

The Science of Cardio

Understanding how cardio training transforms your body

Aerobic Adaptations

Increased Oxygen Utilization

Regular cardio training increases your body's ability to use oxygen more efficiently. Your muscles develop more mitochondria (the "powerhouses" of cells), which helps produce more energy during exercise.

20% Average VO₂ max improvement with 8 weeks of structured cardio

Anaerobic Training Science

Lactate Threshold Improvement

High-intensity interval training enhances your body's ability to clear lactate from the bloodstream. This pushes your anaerobic threshold higher, allowing you to work harder for longer before fatigue sets in.

2-3x More effective at improving lactate threshold than steady-state cardio

Heart Adaptations

Cardiac Hypertrophy

The heart is a muscle that grows stronger with cardio training. Your heart chambers enlarge and cardiac muscle strengthens, allowing more blood to be pumped with each beat (increased stroke volume).

10-20% Increase in stroke volume after 6 months of consistent training

Hormonal Effects

Endorphin Release

During cardio exercise, your body releases endorphins, dopamine, and serotonin - neurotransmitters that reduce pain perception, improve mood, and create the "runner's high" sensation.

30 min Minimum cardio duration for optimal endorphin release
